This class holds the information on how to map a given timeslice to a record row in the cache. It also holds whether or not a given timeslice is dirty and can be used to allocate a new timeslice. This is also exposed as a service for those who need to create timeslices without data being associated to it.

In the future this could also come handy to the same timeslice to multiple entries in the cache.
